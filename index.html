Work Experience

The vast majority of my work experience was gained as a submariner in the United States Navy. More specifically, I was what is called a Fire Control Technician. This, contrary to the speculations of the average interlocutor, has nothing to do with extinguishing infernos of any kind. In fact, firefighting duties and knowledge are required of all submariners. My actual duties involved what one could basically reduce to underwater hunting. I interfaced with Sonar Technicians to track everything that moved around us; the difference between my work and the work of a Sonar Tech was that I was also responsible for the weapons side of the house. I was the one to 'push the button' as my fellow sailors liked to gleefully say. I was required to know the majority of their job as well as all of mine, whereas they were required to be continuously vigilant and were ultimately responsible for maintaining an awareness of the surrounding acoustic picture.
